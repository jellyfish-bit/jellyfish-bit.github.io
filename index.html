About me

Hello, my name is Simon!

I’m a student studying Information Technology in Karlsruhe, Germany. My fascination with coding started when I was twelve, tinkering with Minecraft mods. Since then, I’ve been captivated by coding, delving into different facets of it. Whether it’s crafting websites, developing software, experimenting with new technologies, or exploring the new world of Artificial Intelligence, I’m always eager to learn and expand my skills. Now that I’ve finished school and have my Abitur, I can finally do what I like best. Studying here in Karlsruhe is the perfect environment for me to dive deeper into my passion for coding, technology and AI. It’s an exciting journey that I’m eager to embark on.

As I journey through my studies, I’m driven by the belief that coding has the power to shape the future, and I’m eager to be a part of that journey. I envision myself contributing to innovative projects that push the boundaries of technology and make a meaningful impact on society. With each line of code I write, I’m not just building programs; I’m contributing to a future where technology enhances lives and drives progress. I’m thrilled to see where this journey takes me.
